My Story

I started my journey in Washington, DC, building my first company at 19. Since then, I've founded six startups across different industries.

In 2015, I moved to Copenhagen to lead marketing departments for Planday (acquired), GenieBelt (acquired), Actimo (acquired), and Valuer (IPO) to them scale and expand internationally. That experience cemented my belief in "Growth Hacking" not as a buzzword, but as a systematic process of rapid experimentation.

Over the following 4.5 years, I ran Klint Marketing, an award winning digital growth agency working with large organisations to pay the bills while picking up blockchain and crypto projects for the thrills.

These days, I’m exclusively focused on building blockchain projects with smart and interesting people from around the world.
