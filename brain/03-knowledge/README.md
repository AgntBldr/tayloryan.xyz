# Knowledge domains

Functional domains for the online businesses. **Type lives in frontmatter**; this tree is the
**domain** axis. Sub-folders are created lazily when a node needs one.

- `product/` - research * roadmap * design-ux * development
- `marketing/` - content * seo * paid-ads * email * social * brand
- `sales/` - funnel * pricing * outreach * crm
- `customers/` - icp-personas * support * feedback
- `growth/` - acquisition * retention * metrics * experiments
- `operations/` - tooling * processes * automation
- `finance/` - revenue * costs * unit-economics
- `strategy/` - positioning * competitors * market

Scope a node to a business with `tagged_with: ["[[pil-<business>]]"]` (knowledge is shared
across businesses; the tag filters to one).
