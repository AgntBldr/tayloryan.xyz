The output PDF files are saved in the 'Ref Docs/Archived_PDFs' directory. The naming convention for these files uses the first 7 words of the article title followed by ' - Taylor [PDF].pdf'. The script also updates the CSV to mark 'PDF Saved' as 'Yes' upon successful completion.
