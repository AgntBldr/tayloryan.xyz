The Partners section uses a dark background with a marquee or infinite scroll logo strip for Tier 1 partners like Avalanche, OKX, and CoinMarketCap. Below the marquee, there is a static 'Live on' chain row displaying supported chains like peaq, Avalanche, Base, and BNB Chain. Logos are rendered in monochrome or white on the dark background.
