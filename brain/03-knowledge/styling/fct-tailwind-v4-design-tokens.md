Design tokens are configured in `app/globals.css` using Tailwind v4's `@theme` directive. Defined colors include `neutral-5` for dark backgrounds, `brand-blue`, `brand-yellow`, `brand-teal-1` for light hero backgrounds, and `neutral-2` for muted text. The font family is PPNeueMontreal (Book/Medium/Bold), sourced from `/public/fonts/`.
